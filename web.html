NZ Tourism Application

Task: The task is to create a single page accomdation quoting application, based on how many nights, and how many people. The second part of the task is to display meal options for the chosen accomodation given.

Client: Tourism New Zealand are an organisation who market New Zealand to the world as a tourist destination. They use the 100% Pure Green New Zealand as their main marketing campaign. Tourism New Zealand is responsible for ensuring that New Zealand remains attractive internationally as a visitor destination.

Target Audience: The application is intended to be used by tourists who are looking at coming over to New Zealand for travel. The target audience for this brief is very broad and there is no specific age group or genderthat the app is being marketed towards.

Hand coded application, using HTML, SCSS, Javascript, and Adobe Illustrator.

Black Estate Website

Task: To rebuild and redesign Black Estate's website using content and assets from the original website, as the client was not happy with the original layout.

Problems with original site: Poor organisation of information. The layout was not user friendly. The site was not appealing for the desired target audience. The brand was not being represented well.

Target Audience: Middle aged couples and people who enjoy the finer things in life. Middle to upper class. The client also wanted to broaden this to other age and ethnic groups.

Hand coded website, using HTML, SCSS and Javascript.
